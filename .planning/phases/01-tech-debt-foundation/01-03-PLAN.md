<objective>
Wrap the two blocking synchronous calls in app.py — embed_and_store() and answer() — with
asyncio.to_thread() so they execute in a thread pool instead of blocking the Chainlit event
loop. Also wrap delete_document() consistently. This is a three-line change in app.py.

Purpose: Fixes TECH-01. Without this, any document upload or RAG query freezes the entire
Chainlit process — no other user action can be processed until the call returns.
Output: app.py with three asyncio.to_thread() wrappers. test_app_async.py turns green.
</objective>
